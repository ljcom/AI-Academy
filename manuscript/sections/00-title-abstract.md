Abstract
Recent advances in conversational artificial intelligence have transformed AI systems into powerful knowledge engines capable of answering a wide range of learner questions. While this unprecedented accessibility of information offers new opportunities for self-directed learning, it also introduces challenges related to superficial understanding and the illusion of mastery. Conversational interactions with AI often follow a simple question–answer pattern that does not necessarily lead to structured learning progression.
This chapter proposes the concept of an AI Academy Engine, a framework that reimagines conversational AI not merely as an information provider but as a guided learning system. In this approach, learners begin with a common conversational entry point and are guided through adaptive learning pathways, including dialogue-based exploration, real-world observation, visual explanation, or problem-solving challenges. The AI system supports reflective dialogue, evaluates conceptual understanding, and determines readiness for progression.
The proposed framework highlights the potential of conversational AI to evolve from a knowledge access tool into a structured learning environment supporting mastery-based learning. Ethical considerations related to learner data, cognitive profiling, and privacy are also discussed.
